The Super Mario Index


The Super Mario Index contains the most recognizable characters in the franchise! Like who? Of course we have the 2 Mario Brothers, Mario and Luigi, damsel in distress Princess Peach, the terrifying King of Koopas, Bowser, Mario's companion Yoshi, and Mario's rival Donkey Kong! These characters contributed to the success of the games and influenced generations to a whole new level, standing strong at 43 years!



Character
Description

Mario
Mario Stats

Mario is the main character of the franchise.

He's always bright and cheerful, and is most notable for his outfit: blue overalls, red shirt and cap, and of course, his moustache.

Abilities

Mario has a wide range of power-ups and boosts, such as the ability to throw fire from his hands, swivel into the air with a helicopter cap, slide on the floor on his belly as a penguin, swim like a frog, and many more.

Luigi
Luigi Stats

Luigi is the younger but taller brother of Mario.

In contrast to Mario, Luigi is the shy and timid one of the brothers, afraid of everything, especially ghosts.

Abilities

Unlike Mario, Luigi is taller and can jump higher. However in some of the games, especially Super Mario Bros 2 in Japan or Super Mario Bros. The Lost Levels, he is slippery to play with, as he is more sensitive in walking, giving a more difficult challenge.

As with his brother, he shares the same power-ups and boosts.

Princess Peach
Princess Peach Stats

Princess Peach is the ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom.

The princess who always gets captured and is usually the damsel in distress, Peach is the kind and caring ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom who cares for her citizens, the Toads.

Abilities

Peach is only playable in a few games, as she usually gets kidnapped, allowing the plot in the games to happen, but that doesn't mean she has no power-ups in her artillery.

She can turn into a cat, a tanooki, and use her umbrella to float safely to the ground. In New Super Mario Bros U Deluxe, a more enhanced version of Peach can be played, by getting the crown power-up as Toadette.

Bowser
Bowser Stats

Bowser, or King Koopa, is the main antagonist of the franchise.

He has two objectives: Kidnap and marry Princess Peach, and take over the Mushroom Kingdom. Although he plans ambitiously, he is always defeated by Mario.

Abilities

Bowser is one the most powerful enemies in the franchise, making him a very dangerous threat. In his selection, he can spit fireballs and even breathe fire!

Wanna make things worse? Usually after defeating Bowser in the newer games, he returns from the lava as a skeleton form. But what puts every ability to shame is when... he turns into a HUGE version of himself!

Yoshi
Yoshi Stats

Yoshi is a green dinosaur who accompanies Mario on his adventures.

He can hatch in different colors and those represent his powers and abilities.

Abilities

Yoshi is unique when it comes to jumping. He can flutter jump which allows him to stay in the air for a bit longer period upon jumping. With different colors, it allows Yoshi to pull off different abilities. Red makes Yoshi spit out fireballs. Yellow makes him cause a stomp, and many more.

Donkey Kong
Donkey Kong Stats

Donkey Kong is a large ape and the rival to Mario along with the Wario Bros.

Donkey Kong is the son of the old Donkey Kong, Cranky Kong, who appeared in the arcades. He is a very big showboat, with his own specialized tie, and loves throwing barrels at Mario.

Abilities

While not as dangerous as the King of Koopas, DK has a supposedly unlimited amount of barrels in his disposal, to throw mainly at Mario. He is strong too, but again, not as strong as Bowser they're on two different levels.
